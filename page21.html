Health Care

Health Care organizations are rapidly changing landscapes of assets, surgical supplies, surgical tools, documents, patients, staff, visitors and similar objects. Medical Centers commonly pay excessive rental fees or suffer excessive capital expenditures to ensure the availability of the assets, items and personnel required for the services and surgeries provided to patients and clients on a day-to-day basis. Active and UHF RFID technologies are being embraced by medical centers worldwide for ‘real time’ tracking of assets, supplies, people and other objects, bringing real-time payback through improved efficiencies, avoidance of rentals, reduce capital expenditures and avoidance of obsolescence of critical medical devices and implements utilized for cardiac, orthopedic and similar surgeries.
