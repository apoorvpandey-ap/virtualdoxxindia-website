 Military Forces

 SmartTraxx incorporates substantive knowledge from implementing electronic form solutions since the 1990's.
An important key to leveraging computers for accomplishing eRecords, is to avoid the creation of documents in the first place. Rather than perform tasks using paper documents and scanning the paper after it is created, simply re-engineer business process and transactions utilizing electronic forms.
SmartTraxx can incorporate unlimited electronic form templates, and, can aggregate sets of forms based on the transaction to be completed. SmartTraxx can print individual forms or groups of forms based on the type of workflow occurring.
Enhance productivity and reduce costs by eliminating paper-based business processes.
